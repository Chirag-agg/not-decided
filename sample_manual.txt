MAINTENANCE MANUAL: MTR-999

1. SAFETY WARNING
Before inspecting the motor, ensure lockout-tagout procedures are followed.

2. TROUBLESHOOTING
If excessive vibration is detected, check the bearing alignment.
In case of overheating, verify that cooling fluid from HX-301 is reaching the assembly.

3. INCIDENTS
Reference historical incident INC-25-001 for known bearing degradation patterns.
